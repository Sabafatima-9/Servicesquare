Dishwasher Repair & Maintenance

Common Issues

  • Dishwasher not draining or filling
  • Poor cleaning or residue left on dishes
  • Leaking or pooling water
  • Noisy operation or pump failure
  • Heating element faults or error codes

Maintenance Tips

  • Clean filters and spray arms monthly
  • Run a vinegar or dishwasher-cleaner cycle every 1–2 months
  • Check door seals and hinges for leaks
  • Use recommended detergents and rinse aids

When to Call a Professional

If you experience persistent leaks, electrical issues, heating failures, strange noises, or error codes, our certified technicians can diagnose and repair the problem safely and quickly. We carry common spare parts and provide clear pricing.

Why Professional Servicing Matters

Proper servicing prevents water damage, improves efficiency, extends appliance life, and ensures safe operation. Our team follows manufacturer guidelines and uses quality parts.
